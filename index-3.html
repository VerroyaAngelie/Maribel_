Health Protocol
Posted by Admin

Wear a mask or face covering in common areas and avoid touching your face. Practice physical distancing. Cover your cough or sneeze with your elbow or a tissue then throw that tissue in the trash. Disinfect used surfaces and frequently touched objects. Wash your hands often with soap and water for at least 20 seconds. Use an alcohol-based hand sanitizer with at least 60% alcohol if soap and water are not readily available. Stay home if you are feeling sick and avoid close contact with people who are sick.
