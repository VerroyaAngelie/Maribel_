Beach & Resorts

Coral Beach Club

The Coral Beach Club now has 25 large & comfortable rooms, all maintained to a high standard, a recently refurbished swimming pool and a bar-restaurant & garden area that can easily accommodate up to 100 guests.

The Coral Beach Club has maintained its strong ties with the Calatagan Golf Club (just 15 minutes drive away), and many guests stay here & play there whilst taking advantage of the preferential rates provided as one of the benefits of being a guest of the Coral Beach Club.

Matabungkay Beach Hotel

The ideal resort getaway for family and friends is just two hours away from Manila! Located in the seaside town of Lian, Batangas, Matabungkay Beach Hotel has been a top destination for local and foreign tourists wanting to get away from it all for over 30 years.

With its comfortable accommodations, wide range of outdoor activities, pampering services, and music and sports lounge, Matabungkay provides the total resort experience.

Bella Beach Resort

Bella Beach Resort is an intimate owner run resort located on the white sand beaches of Matabungkay in the Batangas region of the Philippines. Approximately 120km from Manila, Matabungkay and Bella Beach Resort is a favourite for a weekend getaway with both locals and and visitors to the Philippines.

Bella Beach resort promises you a tranquil and relaxing stay with utmost privacy and comfort, giving you the ultimate island experience that everyone has been longing for.
